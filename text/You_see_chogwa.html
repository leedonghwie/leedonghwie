You see1

/ Jaewon Che
translated by donghwi lee

I vapor away again
unknowingly a poem,
I slowly for you to

see

a work only quick but rashest
mostly by who reads
Eat
this fresh bread I bake and

see

my piece
would you
today

(from what you wan do wit me?, Minumsa, 2021)

  1. ‘시’(poem, poetry) sounds si. ↩
